# Product Requirement Document — TuVi

## Purpose
TuVi is a traffic acquisition platform using SEO content.

## Role in Ecosystem
Acquire anonymous visitors and convert them into AnMenh users.

---

## Core Features

### 1. SEO Articles

Types:
- daily horoscope
- zodiac explanations
- compatibility guides

No login required.

---

### 2. Article Structure

Each article must include:

- Title
- Content
- FAQ section
- CTA block

CTA:

"Create your destiny profile for personalized insights"

---

### 3. Conversion Funnel

Flow:

Article → CTA → AnMenh Profile Creation

---

### 4. Internal Linking

Articles auto-link to:
- element pages
- zodiac pages
- related content
