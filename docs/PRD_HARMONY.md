# Product Requirement Document — Harmony Web (Brand Layer)

## Product Name
Harmony Web (Landing & Brand Hub)

## Purpose
To serve as the "Trust Layer" for the entire ecosystem. It explains the philosophical foundation of Harmony, establishing credibility and transferring that trust to the AnMenh product.

## The "Why"
Astrology apps can sometimes be perceived as gimmicky or unscientific. Harmony Web exists to counter this by highlighting the rigorous philosophical roots of Eastern astrology (Ngũ Hành, Bát Tự) combined with modern, data-driven AI systems.

## Target Audience
- Curious skeptics who need to understand "how it works" before signing up.
- Investors, partners, and media looking for the brand story.
- Users exploring the Harmony methodology.

## Core Features (V1)

### 1. Hero Section
- High-impact visuals, premium design aesthetics ("Zen", minimal, rich typography).
- Statement of purpose: *Harmony - Nơi triết lý cổ đại gặp gỡ AI hiện đại.*

### 2. The Philosophy (Trang Triết Lý)
- Explains the methodology: Not magic, but a statistical and philosophical mapping of time, space, and human potential based on Eastern tenets.
- Visual breakdowns of the Bazi (Tứ Trụ) / Zi Wei (Tử Vi) concepts in easy, digestible modern formats.

### 3. Ecosystem Hub
- Navigates visitors via Clear CTAs:
  - -> *Read daily insights* (routes to `tuvi-web`)
  - -> *Discover your destiny* (routes to `anmenh-web`)

### 4. Trust Markers
- Testimonials, methodology accuracy transparency, privacy promises (Commitment to NOT selling user birth data).

## Non Goals
- No user dashboard here.
- No heavy daily horoscope content here (belongs to TuVi).
